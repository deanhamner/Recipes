Famous Chicken & Dumplings

How to Prepare this goodness:

Ingredients:

  • Engligh Peas
  • Chopped Carrots
  • Chopped Onion
  • Chicken Broth
  • Cream of Mushroom
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Can of Puilsbury Buscuits

As true southers, we don't measure out our ingredients. We just add more til it feels right. It'll be different for everyone. depending on which ingredients they like the most.

Prep:

  1. Defrost Chicken
  2. Flatten each buicuit, and cut into several slices with a pizza cutter
  3. Boil Chicken with Bones in
  4. Once thoroughly cookied, remove chiken and cut off meat
  5. Add broth, cream of mushroom, and some of the boiling water to a pot
  6. Add all other ingredients. Stir continuously, not letting anything stick to the bottom
  7. After 20 minutes, it'll be very thick. At that point, let it cool and enjoy
