Pepperoni Quesadila

How to Prepare this goodness:

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Tortilla Wraps
  • Chopped Onion
  • Your favorite cheese
  • Pepperoni

As true southers, we don't measure out our ingredients. We just add more til it feels right. It'll be different for everyone. depending on which ingredients they like the most.

Prep:

  1. Heat stovetop to medium-low
  2. Apply butter to pan and melt
  3. Apply cheese, onion, and Pepperoni to half of the Tortilla
  4. Place Tortilla on pan
  5. Cover pan. Watch. Once cheese starts to melt, fold the Tortilla and flip
  6. Continue to flip and cover such that the insides are melty and the outside is golden-brown
  7. Let cool and enjoy
