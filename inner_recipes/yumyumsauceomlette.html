Odd Omlette

How to Prepare this goodness:

Ingredients:

  • Your favorite omlette recipe!
  • Ym-Yum Sauce
  • Hot Sauce

As true southerners, we don't measure out our ingredients. We just add more til it feels right. It'll be different for everyone. depending on which ingredients they like the most.

Prep:

  1. Follow your favorite omlette recipe
  2. Apply dab of hot sauce and yum yum sauce before you fold the omlette
  3. Cook until sauce begins to leak out
  4. Enjoy this surprisingly good aboniaiton!
